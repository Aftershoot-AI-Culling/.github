# Aftershoot AI Culling

**Aftershoot AI Culling** is an intelligent photo selection and editing platform designed specifically for professional photographers. As a powerful **Aftershoot Photo Selection** tool, it uses computer vision to analyze composition, focus, expression, and closed eyes, then automatically categorizes images as "Good," "Maybe," or "Reject." This **Aftershoot Image Editing** platform applies consistent color grading, exposure adjustments, and tone curves across entire photo sets using personalized AI models trained on your editing style. With **Aftershoot Workflow Tool**, you can seamlessly export selections to Lightroom Classic, Lightroom CC, or Capture One with metadata, ratings, and color labels intact.
